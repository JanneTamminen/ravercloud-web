10 / Questions

Before you apply.

Do I need VR?

No. RaverCloud is a screen-based virtual club — your fans watch and join from a browser. There's no headset required for you or your audience.

Do I need OBS?

Yes — for now, you'll stream into the virtual club using OBS. We give you a guided setup and a 10-minute tech rehearsal so it's straightforward even if it's your first time.

Can I perform from home?

Absolutely. A home setup is the most common way founding DJs run their residency. You bring the decks and the camera; we provide the room.

Can I sell tickets?

Yes. Each event page supports paid entry, tiers, and tips. Replay access can be sold or gated after the show ends.

Can fans appear in the virtual club?

Yes — fans can opt in to the audience video wall and appear inside the room. It's always opt-in, never forced.

What happens after the live event?

Your set becomes a replay you own. Gate it, sell it, clip it for promo, or release it free — every show builds your archive.

Is this fully self-serve?

Not yet. The early version is a guided Founding DJ program — we set things up with you by hand so the first cohort's shows look and feel premium. Self-serve tooling comes later.
